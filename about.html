About Us

Our Committment

We are dedicated to delivering excellence in every aspect of our business. How We Do It

From Farm to table

We take pride in our farm-to-table approach, ensuring that every ingredient is sourced responsibly and ethically. By partnering with local farmers and artisans, we support our community while delivering fresh, flavorful products straight to your table.

Our Terroir

Our terroir is the heart and soul of our operation. Nestled in the picturesque countryside, our vineyards benefit from unique soil compositions and microclimates, resulting in wines of exceptional character and complexity.

Our Family

As a family-owned and operated business, we cherish the values of tradition, craftsmanship, and togetherness. With generations of expertise passed down, we infuse each product with the warmth and passion that only a family can provide.

Renewable Energy

Committed to sustainability, we harness the power of renewable energy to minimize our environmental footprint. From solar panels to energy-efficient practices, we strive to protect the planet for future generations while crafting the finest products for our customers.

The Process

Types of Aging

At Francini Wineries, we employ various aging techniques to craft wines of unparalleled quality and complexity. Each method of aging imparts unique characteristics to the wine, resulting in a diverse range of styles to suit every palate.

Oak Barrel Aging

Our oak barrel aging process adds depth and structure to our wines, with subtle nuances of vanilla, spice, and toasted oak. We carefully select French and American oak barrels to complement the flavors of each grape variety, allowing for gradual integration and refinement.

Bottle Aging

We believe in the art of bottle aging to allow our wines to evolve gracefully over time. By carefully cellaring our wines in optimal conditions, we encourage the development of complex aromas and flavors, resulting in wines of exceptional depth and character.
