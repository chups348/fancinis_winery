Menu

Pizza

Mediterranean

A delightful blend of Mediterranean flavors with tangy olives, sun-dried tomatoes, and feta cheese atop a crispy crust.

Mushroom & Goat Cheese

Earthy and savory, our Mushroom pizza features a generous medley of fresh mushrooms, garlic, goat cheese, and aromatic herbs.

Classic

Timeless and satisfying, our Classic pizza boasts a perfect balance of zesty tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings.

Pies

Spanakopita

A Greek classic, our Spanakopita pie is a flaky pastry filled with spinach, feta cheese, and fragrant herbs, offering a taste of the Mediterranean.

Hearty and flavorful, our Spinach and Beef pie combines tender beef, spinach, and melted cheese, wrapped in golden pastry.

Sweet and savory unite in our Goat Cheese and Berries pie, featuring creamy goat cheese, tangy berries, and a hint of honey drizzle.

Indulge in the rich, velvety texture of our Creme Brulee pastry, featuring a smooth custard base and caramelized sugar crust.

Custard

Delicate and comforting, our Custard pastry is filled with creamy vanilla custard, encased in buttery, flaky pastry layers.

Tarts

Our assortment of Tarts offers a burst of fruity flavors, from tangy lemon to luscious berries, nestled in a crisp, buttery pastry shell.
